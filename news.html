3D Printing News & Breakthroughs

Stay up-to-date on the exciting advancements happening in the world of 3D printing!

Medical Marvels

  • First 3D-Printed Eye Implanted in Human Patient: A groundbreaking surgery in London used a 3D-printed eye to restore partial vision in a patient.
  • Custom Cataract Lenses Revolutionize Eye Surgery: Researchers developed a 3D printing technique to create customized lenses for cataract surgery, improving outcomes.
  • 3D-Printed Ribcage Saves Cancer Patient's Life: A Spanish patient received a world-first transplant using a 3D-printed ribcage, demonstrating the technology's life-saving potential.

Aerospace & Beyond

  • Europe's New Rocket Takes 3D Printing to Space: The upcoming launch of the Ariane 6 rocket utilizes 3D-printed components, marking a leap forward in space exploration.
  • Airbus A320 Fleet Upgraded with 3D-Printed Parts: Finnair is incorporating 3D-printed components into its Airbus A320 airplanes, showcasing the technology's role in modern aviation.

Other Cool Developments

  • AI Powers 3D Model Generation: Meshy's innovative platform utilizes AI to streamline the creation of 3D models for printing, making the process more accessible.
  • Researchers Explore Bioprinting with Plant Cells: A new bioprinting method using plant cells opens doors for sustainable and biocompatible materials in 3D printing.
